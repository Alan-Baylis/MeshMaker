Tips and tricks

  • Modeling is simpler from reference, add plane to scene and attach sketch image to it via Texture Browser.
  • Flip edges for better topology with ⇧F.
  • Subdivided icosahedron has better topology than simple sphere.
  • Add more triangles with split ⇧S in edges or triangles mode.
  • You can enable "soft selection" in Edit menu, which is better for clay or organic modeling with higher triangle count.
